NestLedger Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

NestLedger ("we," "our," or "us") is a family budgeting app that helps households track shared expenses, budget plans, and shopping lists. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Email address for authentication (via Supabase Auth)
  • Profile Data: Display name, avatar emoji, and family/household name that you choose to create
  • Budget Data: Budget plan names, amounts, start/end dates
  • Expense Data: Expense amounts, categories, dates, descriptions, and who paid/used
  • Shopping List Data: Item names, quantities, categories, and purchase status
  • Borrow/Repay Data: Records of money borrowed from or repaid to the family budget
  • Push Token: Device token for sending notifications (only if you enable notifications)

3. What Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Exact location data (GPS)
  • Contacts from your device
  • Camera or microphone access
  • Photos or media files
  • Device identifiers for advertising
  • Analytics or tracking data

4. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide NestLedger's core functionality:

  • Authenticate your account and personalize your experience
  • Sync your budget data across family members
  • Calculate remaining balances and track spending
  • Send notifications when family members add expenses or mark shopping items as bought
  • Provide real-time sync across all household members

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to power NestLedger:

  • Supabase - Database, authentication, and real-time sync.
    https://supabase.com/privacy
  • Expo - Push notification delivery.
    https://expo.dev/privacy

These providers are GDPR-compliant and have their own privacy policies.

6. Data Sharing

We never:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers or ad networks
  • Use your data for marketing without consent
  • Transfer your data to third parties except as needed for app functionality (e.g., Supabase for authentication)

7. Data Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted in transit)
  • Supabase provides Row-Level Security (RLS) - only family members can see family data
  • Authentication is handled securely by Supabase Auth
  • Push tokens are stored securely and never shared

8. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can:

  • Delete your account - removes all your profile data
  • Delete individual items - expenses, shopping items, budget plans
  • Disable push notifications - revokes your device token

9. Your Rights

Under GDPR and similar regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing for specific purposes

To exercise these rights, delete your account through the app or contact us below.

10. Children's Privacy

NestLedger is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • Post a notice in the app (for material changes)

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us.
